## What home warranties may cover

A home warranty may help pay for covered repairs or replacement when normal wear and tear knocks out a listed item. The key word is **listed**. If the contract does not name it, assume it is not invited to the party.

## What home warranties usually do not cover

Most plans exclude improper installation, code upgrades, cosmetic defects, pre-existing conditions, and damage from neglect. Some also cap how much they will pay. That cap matters when the repair bill arrives wearing steel-toed boots.

## Read the fine print before buying

Look for the service call fee, waiting period, coverage limit, maintenance requirements, and who chooses the contractor. A useful plan is boring in the right places and clear about the expensive places.

## Coverage varies by plan and provider

Two plans with similar names can behave very differently. Compare sample contracts before you decide. Your future self, currently standing in a puddle, will appreciate it.
